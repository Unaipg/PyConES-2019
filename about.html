About Us

PyConES is the most important national Python conference in Spain. PyConES will be held for the seventh time from 04 to 09 October 2019 in Alicante, after six successful editions in Madrid, Zaragoza, Valencia, Almería, Cáceres and Málaga, following the format of the worldwide PyCon event.
